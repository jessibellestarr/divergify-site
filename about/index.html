About Divergify

Divergify is a neurodivergent-first productivity companion for ADHD brains, autistic pattern-seekers, trauma survivors, and everyone else who’s tired of being told to “just try harder.”

Instead of forcing you into a rigid system, Divergify adapts to your mood, energy, and capacity. It’s part sidekick, part coach, part chaos translator.

We believe your brain isn’t the problem. The tools are.
