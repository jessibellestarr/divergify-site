B

Brain Bounce

Brain Bounce captures that lively, rapid-fire attention shift where your mind springs between ideas with electric momentum. With practice, it becomes a superpower: channel it into short, timed sessions or note captures to harness the bounce without exhaustion. Embrace it as a source of vitality that turns what others call distraction into a fountain of creativity. With gentle structure, Brain Bounce becomes a controlled rhythm that propels you forward.

Brain Buffer

Brain Buffer involves intentionally scheduling cushion time before and after intense activities to decompress and recalibrate, acting as a protective layer against cognitive overload. This is crucial for neurodivergents who process stimuli deeply, preventing burnout by providing space for transitions.

Back to top

Brainstorm Sprint

A Brainstorm Sprint is a high-speed, time-bound session dedicated to generating ideas rapidly—capturing brilliance before it fades. Set a 10–15 minute timer and let thoughts flow without judgment, aiming for quantity over quality to leverage natural creativity surges. Follow with a quick sort to refine the gems and turn them into action.

C

Calm Cache

Calm Cache is a personal reservoir of calming elements—sounds, scents, textures, or visuals—to draw from when overwhelm spikes. Keep it accessible and varied. This habit provides quick regulation options to quiet static and restore clarity.

Circuit Break Breath

Circuit Break Breath is a concise breathing exercise designed to interrupt overwhelm—like cutting the power to escalating stress and resetting your system. It’s a quick tool to regain control during sensory or emotional spikes.

D

Daydream Dock

Daydream Dock is a designated slot where unstructured mind-wandering is not only allowed—it’s encouraged. Time-box it and capture anything interesting. Channel natural drift into productive insight without guilt or derailment.

Divergent Debrief

Divergent Debrief is a quick reflection after you ship or stop. Capture what worked for your brain, what didn’t, and what to try next. This compounding insight accelerates growth and eases future starts.

F

Focus Campfire

Focus Campfire is a recurring accountability gathering—co-working or check-ins—where shared warmth keeps momentum alive. Harness community to sustain effort and make progress feel social and safe.

Focus Sprint

A Focus Sprint is an intense, time-boxed interval (e.g., 25 minutes) tuned to your attention rhythm, followed by a rest. It builds endurance without burnout and respects natural cycles.

H

Hyperfocus Haven

Hyperfocus Haven is a bounded space to celebrate deep immersion safely. Use timers and exit ramps to enjoy intensity while avoiding overrun and crash.

Idea Avalanche

Idea Avalanche is the thrilling yet overwhelming cascade of concepts. Capture first—no sorting—then categorize quickly. Prevent burial and turn chaos into structured innovation.

Mindful Momentum

Mindful Momentum advances with energy awareness. Check in before you push, pivot, or pause so you keep pace without slipping into autopilot or guilt.

N

Neurodivergent Nest

Neurodivergent Nest is a tailored environment that makes your brain feel secure and productive. Customize for your senses—light, sound, touch—so work feels inviting and doable.

P

Procrastination Pivot

Procrastination Pivot turns avoidance into curiosity. Ask, “What would make this interesting?” or “What’s a 60-second starter?” Curiosity unlocks the first move.

S

Sensory Menu

Sensory Menu is a ready list of inputs for different needs—up regulate, down regulate, or steady. Customize and keep it accessible to enhance adaptability.

Synapse Sync

Synapse Sync is the alignment of interests, energy, and support—those peak windows when everything clicks. Spot and engineer them intentionally to maximize potential.

Thought Constellation

Thought Constellation maps interconnected ideas so you can choose a direction. Visual connections reveal patterns, show gaps, and spark next steps.

Time Shield

Time Shield protects your focus blocks with boundaries—calendar holds, do-not-disturb, and social agreements. Guard them like appointments with your future.
