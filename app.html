Beta mode

Test-driving Divergify

This is the experimental, honest, slightly feral version of Divergify. Things will move, break, and improve based on how real people actually use it.

Planned ways to access the beta:

  • Progressive Web App (PWA): install from your browser to your home screen.
  • Android APK: direct download from this page for early testers.

We’re not in the app stores yet on purpose. We want to build this with our community first, then scale it.

Step 1: Join the beta list

Get updates, access links, and patch notes as we roll out features.

Step 2: Install

Once we flip the switch:

  • iOS: “Add to Home Screen” from Safari (PWA).
  • Android: Install PWA or sideload APK (we’ll walk you through it).
