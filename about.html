Why Divergify exists

We’re not fixing you. We’re fixing the systems.

Divergify was born out of exhaustion — the kind that comes from trying to jam a loud, nonlinear, brilliant brain into tools built for quiet, linear, neurotypical workflows.

Instead of forcing neurodivergent people to contort themselves around rigid systems, we’re building tools that flex around real human brains: ADHD, autism, trauma, sensory overwhelm, executive dysfunction and all.

We pair research on executive function, dopamine, and sensory load with practical experiments in the wild. The result: systems that don’t rely on willpower alone, but on smart scaffolding, gentle nudges, and honest feedback.

Humans + AI

AI is not the replacement. It’s the bridge.

Neurodivergent brains are high-bandwidth. The problem isn’t the ideas; it’s holding all the threads at once without burning out.

Divergify uses AI to:

  • Catch ideas before they vanish.
  • Turn spirals into step-by-step plans.
  • Remember the boring details so you don’t have to.
  • Give you structure without taking your autonomy.

We believe the future looks like humans + AI working together, not humans being graded by machines. Divergify is our way of making that future feel possible, ethical, and actually usable.
